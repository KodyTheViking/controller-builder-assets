How to Use the Builder

1. Select your controller model.
2. Choose a headstock style if available.
3. Pick your base color, inlay style, and pickguard color.
4. If the visualizer doesn't reflect the selections you chose, click them again. It has a hard time updating the headstock.
5. When finished, enter your name and email and click Send Build.

Important: Use the same name and email when placing your order on the store so we can match your build configuration.

Colors shown in the builder are digital previews and may vary slightly from the final product.
