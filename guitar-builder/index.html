How to Use the Builder

1. Pick your body shape and color.
2. Pick a headstock shape and color.
3. Pick from the available inlays and color them.
4. Pick your accent detail. This will be pickguards or stripes. Some models don't offer anything here.
5. Want a color that's not shown here? Leave your idea in the Notes field of your build submission and I will contact you if I need more information!
6. When finished, fill in the form submission with your info and click Send Build.

Important: Use the same name and email when placing your order on the store so we can match your build configuration.

Colors shown in the builder are digital previews and may vary slightly from the final product.
